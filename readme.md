# Skill Level Up v2.0 - TTRPG Engine

A lightweight, web-based character management system and mechanical calculator for the custom "Skill Level up" tabletop role-playing game. It automatically handles the complex math for combat, magic scaling, and resource tracking so players can focus on the story. Version 2.0 introduces a complete visual overhaul, bringing a highly polished, modern game-interface design.

## Features

* **Dynamic Theming Engine:** Choose from 6 unique visual styles—including *Grimoire Academy*, *Hallowed Mist*, *Industrial Grunge*, and *Tavern Tabletop*. Every theme comes with fully customized Light and Dark modes.
* **Modern Interface & Animations:** Enjoy a sleek glassmorphism aesthetic with frosted panels, fast cone-shaped critical hit flashes, and custom game-style UI notifications.
* **Live Resource Tracking:** Interactive HP, Stamina, and Mana pools that automatically calculate their maximum limits based on your Constitution, Endurance, and Mana Supply. 
* **Automated Combat Math:** Instantly calculates melee/magic damage, dodge values, hit success, and your action economy using your skill points and the current d20 roll.
* **Zero Installation:** Built entirely with plain HTML, CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript. No servers or databases required.

## How to Run

1. Save `index.html`, `style.css`, and `script.js` into the same folder on your computer.
2. Double-click `index.html` to open the application in any modern web browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, etc.).
3. Open the sidebar to select your preferred theme, enter your skill points, and watch your character's stats generate in real-time.
